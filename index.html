Hey there!

I’ve had a chicken as a pet and used to catch lizards like it was my job. I’m a child at heart. I’ve lived in the South my entire life but have traveled the world and experienced many cultures. When playing sports as a child, I aimed for the fences…even if we were playing tennis. I’ve survived hurricanes, water spouts, southern blizzards and the occasional all nighter. I love southern bbq, old cameras, pineapples, dinosaurs and antiques.

I have an abundance of useless knowledge that breaks the ice on any occasion and I’m a wiz at game shows, especially Jeopardy, Wheel of Fortune and The Price is Right.

Above all I have a passion to try new things and to explore.

The popular puzzle game is back. Portal 2’s new two-player mode is insanely hard. To succeed, teams must be completely in tune.

Prior to the game’s release, we’ve created a special demo that allows gamers to find their perfect opposite – another player who can do everything they can’t.

XBOX MARKETPLACE

A free demo is provided to current gamers available on the Xbox marketplace.
After playing a demo, gamers are rated on their strengths and who they should be paired up with for assistance.

INTERACTIVE PORTALS

Digital “portals” are connected to other screens around the country.

The display comes alive when people from multiple locations walk in front of the screen

displaying game information and a co-op trailer.

DIGITAL ESCALATOR BACKS

As someone rides the escalator they see an extra reflection moving alongside theirs.

WALL DEMO

Trailer displays are turned into mini co-op challenges. For the video to play,
two people must perform opposing actions in unison.

Summer Freeze Giveaway

Classroom Hero

Tasked with promoting and helping to boost social media presence with a very small budget and timing, the Classroom Hero was born. Classroom Hero was a Facebook contest, which allows students to nominate a teacher who has been their hero. Then both the submittor and the teacher are entered into the contest this led to additional Little Debbie Facebook contests.

SHAREBOT 2013
